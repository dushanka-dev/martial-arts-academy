History

Martial Arts Academy was born in 1996

We started Martial Arts Academy as a place for new Martial Arts students to have a place where they can train in a safe eneviroment.

Over the year it has grown into a state of the arts facility with over 100 students attend every day to train and become the best Martial Artist thet can be. Our instructors have many years of experience in many Martial Arts disciplines. They love teaching new students and helping them grown physically and mentally.
